### Project 1 — AI Biology Response Evaluation
📁 `project-1-evaluation/sme_evaluation_report.html`

**What it demonstrates:** Ability to validate biological reasoning and identify conceptual errors in AI-generated content — the core skill required for Biology SME / RLHF Evaluator roles.

- 10 UG-level biology questions evaluated across 5 domains

## Research

- Multi-season, multi-site ecological study (Summer, Monsoon, Winter)
- 8 parameters analysed: pH, DO, Turbidity, TDS, BOD, Nitrates, Phosphates, Temperature
